# Privacy Policy for PEDSS App

**Last Updated:** January 2024

## 1. Introduction

PEDSS (Pediatric Emergency Department Seizure Score) is a medical assessment tool developed by AIIMS, New Delhi in collaboration with IIIT Delhi. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Patient Assessment Data
The app collects the following information when you create an assessment:
- Patient name or identification number
- Patient age (in months)
- Patient gender
- Assessment date and time
- Clinical parameters (P, E, D, S1, S2 scores)
- Calculated PEDSS score and risk assessment

### 2.2 User Profile Information (Optional)
If you choose to create a profile:
- Healthcare provider name
- Professional title
- Hospital/institution name
- Contact information (email, phone)

### 2.3 Technical Information
- App version
- Device information (for compatibility purposes)
- Usage statistics (anonymized)

## 3. How We Store Your Data

### 3.1 Local Storage
**All patient data is stored locally on your device only.** We do not transmit, upload, or store any patient information on external servers or cloud services.

- Data is stored in your device's local storage
- No internet connection is required for app functionality
- Data remains on your device until you delete it

### 3.2 Data Export
You have the option to export assessment data:
- Export to CSV or PDF format
- Exported files are saved to your device
- You control where and how exported data is shared

## 4. How We Use Your Information

### 4.1 Primary Purpose
- Calculate PEDSS scores for clinical assessment
- Generate risk assessments and clinical interpretations
- Maintain case history for your reference
- Provide clinical decision support

### 4.2 Data Processing
- All calculations are performed locally on your device
- No data is sent to external servers for processing
- No third-party services are used for data analysis

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 No Automatic Sharing
**We do not share, sell, or disclose your data to any third parties.**

### 5.2 Your Control
- You control all data export and sharing
- You can delete any assessment data at any time
- You can clear all app data through settings

### 5.3 Legal Requirements
We may be required to disclose information only if:
- Required by law or legal process
- Necessary to protect our rights or safety
- In case of medical emergency (as per applicable regulations)

## 6. Data Security

### 6.1 Local Security
- Data is encrypted on your device using standard mobile security
- No network transmission of sensitive data
- Access is controlled by device security (PIN, biometrics, etc.)

### 6.2 Your Responsibility
- Keep your device secure with password/PIN protection
- Do not share your device with unauthorized users
- Export and share data only through secure channels

## 7. Data Retention

### 7.1 Storage Duration
- Data remains on your device until you delete it
- You can delete individual assessments or all data
- Clearing app cache or uninstalling removes all data

### 7.2 Backup
- If you backup your device, assessment data may be included
- We recommend reviewing your device backup settings
- Cloud backups are your responsibility

## 8. Your Rights

### 8.1 Access
- View all stored assessments at any time
- Access case history through the app

### 8.2 Deletion
- Delete individual assessments
- Clear all app data through settings
- Uninstall app to remove all data

### 8.3 Export
- Export assessment data in CSV or PDF format
- Share data as you choose

### 8.4 Correction
- Edit patient information before finalizing assessment
- Update profile information at any time

## 9. Children's Privacy

This app is designed for healthcare professionals to assess pediatric patients. The app does not directly collect information from children. All data entry is performed by authorized healthcare providers.

## 10. Medical Disclaimer

**IMPORTANT:** This app is a clinical decision support tool and should not replace professional medical judgment. 

- PEDSS scores are predictive tools, not definitive diagnoses
- Always consult with qualified medical professionals
- Use this tool as an aid, not a replacement for clinical expertise
- The developers are not liable for clinical decisions made using this app

## 11. Third-Party Services

### 11.1 No Third-Party Analytics
- We do not use third-party analytics services
- We do not use advertising services
- We do not use crash reporting services that collect personal data

### 11.2 App Stores
- App installation through Google Play Store is subject to Google's privacy policy
- We do not control data collection by app stores

## 12. International Users

This app is designed for use by healthcare professionals. If you are using this app outside India, please ensure compliance with local healthcare data protection regulations.

- Significant changes will be notified through app updates
